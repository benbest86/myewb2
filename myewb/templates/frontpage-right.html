African Leader profile
Ajuratu Alurigo is a tomato farmer in Bukere, Northern Ghana, and part of the Women in Extension Volunteers group in the area.
"Now I am trained to help my community grow their produce in a better way. I don't want my community to suffer any more, and I believe that by working together as a united people we can do anything. Someday we will be strong and our communities will prosper."
